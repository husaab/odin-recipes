Hamburger

Description

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Buns
  • Vegetables of Choice (Tomatoes, Cucumber, etc)
  • Ketchup or Sauce of Choice

Steps

  1. Throw a bun onto a plate
  2. Throw meat onto the bun
  3. Throw the cheese slices onto the meat
  4. Throw your vegetables onto the cheese
  5. Put your ketchup on the vegetables
  6. Put the top bun onto the vegetables
  7. BAM! Enjoy your raw burger
